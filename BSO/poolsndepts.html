    ORG COMPOSITION

    OUR POOLS

    Performing Pool

    The orchestra performers is what the organization showcases during its events throughout the year. It is composed of many different kinds of instrumentalists; from string players to pianists, percussionists, woodwind and brass players, guitar and bass guitar players, as well as many others.

    The performing pool aims to develop its members through orchestra-wide rehearsals, sectionals, multiple gigs throughout the year, and the year-end concert.

    Operations Pool

    They serve as the logistics and productions arms of the orchestra. Members will be expected to participate in venue planning, stage productions, lights and sounds, front of house, equipment handling, and program creation. Operations members are also responsible for coordinating the rehearsal venues and times for orchestra practices.

    Communications Pool

    They serve to uphold the visual identity and branding of Blue Symphony Orchestra. Members will be expected to create content for social media, design promo materials for events, and/or document gigs and concerts through photos and videos. Being a member will require a lot of creative thinking and good communication skills with one another.

    OUR DEPARTMENTS

    Finance Department

    They aim to ensure the long standing success of Blue Symphony’s yearly concerts, gigs, and other activities. To this end, members are expected to be organized and punctual in handling the finances and documents that help the organization’s projects remain feasible and profitable.

    External Relations (ExRel) Department

    ExRel upholds the public face of the organization. It manages the relationships of the organization with different sponsors, companies, organizations, and other third-party institutions. People who are adept in social media also play a huge role in promoting exposure and recognition for the organization.

    Internal Relations (IR) Department

    IR ensures good welfare and development for each member in the organisation. Members of this department are expected to facilitate and oversee social projects, individual consultations, member information, as well as to analyze feedback from project evaluations.

    Composers and Arrangers Pool (CAP)

    The Composers and Arrangers Pool (CAP) of Blue Symphony is the creative force behind the musical arrangements the orchestra plays. Members of this department are required to participate in music theory workshops in order to hone their knowledge, interests, and talents in musical arrangement and composition.
