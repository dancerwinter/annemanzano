    WHO WE ARE

    The Blue Symphony Orchestra prides itself on being a student-led orchestra that welcomes all kinds of people who share a passion for instrumental music.

    MISSION - VISION

    As the pioneer orchestra of the Loyola Schools, it envisions itself as a community for Filipino artistry — an organization whose primary focus is the holistic development of its members, deeply rooted in the Ignatian values of excellence and service through musical production and musicianship.

    We aim to hold concerts that showcase musical excellence in contemporary and classic genres, spread awareness and appreciation of orchestral music as an performance art both within and beyond the Loyola Schools, provide opportunities for members to hone their abilities in areas of musical performance and production through the organization’s major semestral events, and ensure the organization’s sustainability through its projects and overarching musical direction.

    OUR FLAGSHIP PROJECTS

    The Year-End Concert

    The concert is the culminating event at the end of the school year where the members come together and utilize the skills that they have learned in order to successfully hold an orchestral production.

    Composers & Arrangers Workshop

    The workshop sessions tackle various music theory topics essential and important to arrangement. Topics range from basic concepts such as note reading to formal analysis and composition techniques.

    The Showcase

    Serving as the main project for the first semester, the Showcase is an avenue for performers, to build up their confidence, and production team, to apply their skills from the workshops, through a culminating recital.
